Department of Computer Technology

The Department of Computer Science

Since 2007, the Department of Computer Science has been offering Higher National Diploma programs, providing students with comprehensive training in various computing fields. This training equips them with the necessary knowledge for the development and utilization of contemporary computing technologies. Graduates are positioned for employment in roles that are in high demand within society.

Currently, accreditation is pending for the following programs:

  • BSc. Computer Science with Data Mining
  • BSc. Web Development and Mobile Computing
