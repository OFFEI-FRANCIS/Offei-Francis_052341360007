Faculty of Applied Sciences & Technology

The Faculty of Applied Sciences and Technology was established by separating it from the original Faculty of Applied Sciences in October 2016. The decision to rename the faculty was motivated by the intention to introduce new programs related to technology and to enhance the effective administration of the existing ones within the institution.
